Latest Update

Update 1.0.7 — Endless AI Story Continuation

The adventure now supports AI-assisted endless chapter generation directly from the story card toolbar.

What’s included:

  • New AI Continue button to generate fresh story chapters on demand so the journey never ends.
  • AI chapter output creates new scenes dynamically and links them into your active timeline history.
  • Backend endpoint /ai/continue-story now returns narrative paragraphs plus branching choice prompts.
  • Each generated scene includes two onward choices, enabling effectively infinite play loops.
