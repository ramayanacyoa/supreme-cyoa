Update 1.0.1 — Family Setup Removed

Family Setup has been fully disabled, and the intro now supports player-name-only start flow.

Feature list:

  • Branching Ramayana choose-your-own-adventure story flow with multiple endings.
  • Name personalization throughout the story with canon fallback defaults.
  • Sibling gender selectors with pronoun-aware story text replacements.
  • Undo, restart, and progression timeline receipt support.
  • Built-in soundtrack controls with cross-page playback state persistence.
  • Dedicated People of the Ramayana page with character and kingdom filters.
