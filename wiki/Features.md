# Features

_Last updated: 2026-04-19 (UTC)_

## Gameplay and UI Features

- Branching Ramayana CYOA narrative with scene-based progression and route-dependent outcomes.
- Undo support for the most recent choice.
- Restart options from ending scenes.
- My Storyline modal that lists visited scenes and includes each scene description.
- Expanded scene writing with deeper narrative descriptions and frequent player-name callouts for immersion.
- Scene dialogue snippets in key moments with speaker labels to highlight what characters are saying during major events.
- Training Ground Trivia minigame sequence unlocked immediately after meeting Hanuman.
- Bharata encounter repositioned to occur right before the Sita abduction sequence.
- Hover/focus helper text on art buttons:
  - Start button: `begin your quest`
  - Undo button: `undo`
- Responsive navigation and secondary lore pages.

- Navbar soundtrack controls now use a simple native audio player in the navbar with muted autoplay, plus play/scrub/volume controls while hiding speed and download options.
- Navbar soundtrack now includes a dedicated volume slider with a live percentage readout, persistent saved volume preference, and resolution.js-driven mobile sizing refinements.
