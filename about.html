About The Sacred Path of Rama

Project Description

The Sacred Path of Rama is a narrative-driven RPG adaptation of the Valmiki Ramayana, built as an interactive web experience. Players shape Rama's journey through strategic choices, companion loyalty, and moral accountability across every major arc, from forest exile to the final chapters of Uttara Kanda.

This project exists to present epic literature as living, participatory storytelling. It combines respectful mythological grounding with modern RPG design so players do not only read the story—they shoulder its consequences.

What makes it unique is its integrated consequence model: personality traits, character affection, combat outcomes, and public perception all interact to alter scenes, dialogue, alliance strength, and endings.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to unite mythology and interactive narrative craft in a format that is both immersive and educational. By preserving core events of the Ramayana while allowing meaningful agency, we encourage players to explore timeless themes— dharma, leadership, sacrifice, justice, and grief—through active decision-making.

Features Overview

  • Branching campaign structure covering Forest, Kishkindha, Lanka War, Return to Ayodhya, and Uttara Kanda.
  • Personality engine tracking Dharma, Aggression, Compassion, Honor, and Strategy.
  • Affection system for major companions with loyalty thresholds and war-time consequences.
  • Multi-phase boss combat design for Vali, Indrajit, Kumbhakarna, and Ravana.
  • Progression layers including inventory, skill growth, camp management, and divine blessings.
  • Multiple endings based on ethical consistency, governance outcomes, and relational trust.
