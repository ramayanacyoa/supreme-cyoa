About This Website

The Ramayana Adventure is an interactive story website where players make choices to experience different paths through a classic epic. The site focuses on readable storytelling, consequence-based decisions, and accessible web design so visitors can learn and play at the same time.

The goal is to turn a timeless text into an engaging journey: every major decision can change character outcomes, scene flow, and endings.

About the Ramayana

The Ramayana is one of India's great Sanskrit epics, traditionally attributed to the sage Valmiki. It follows Prince Rama, Princess Sita, and Lakshmana through exile, loss, loyalty, and war against Ravana of Lanka.

Beyond its central conflict, the Ramayana explores themes of dharma (duty), honor, leadership, compassion, and the consequences of personal choices. Its stories have shaped literature, performing arts, and cultural traditions across South and Southeast Asia for centuries.
